About Us

Greenfield International School is a center for excellence in education, where students are nurtured to become responsible global citizens. Founded in 1998, our institution has consistently delivered high academic standards and a strong emphasis on values, innovation, and leadership.

We provide an inclusive, nurturing, and stimulating environment for students from over 40 nationalities, preparing them for success in a dynamic and interconnected world.

Admissions

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and aim to provide them with a meaningful and enriching academic experience.

  • Online application submission
  • Entrance exam (Grade 1 and above)
  • Parent-student interview
  • Confirmation and enrollment

Academics

Our academic program spans Kindergarten through Grade 12. Each level is designed to build foundational skills, foster intellectual curiosity, and develop critical thinking abilities.

  • Core Subjects: Math, Science, English, Social Studies
  • Languages: Spanish, French, Mandarin
  • Technology Integration: Robotics, Programming, AI
  • Creative Arts: Music, Theater, Visual Arts
  • Physical Education and Wellness Programs
